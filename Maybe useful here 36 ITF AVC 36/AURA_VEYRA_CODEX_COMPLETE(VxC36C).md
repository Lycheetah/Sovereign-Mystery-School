#### The Compression Score

Not all blocks on the pyramid are equally stable. Stability depends on:

```
C = E × P
```

Where:
- **E** = Evidence (how much data supports this)
- **P** = Power (explanatory force)

**Examples:**

- P = High (explained motion, gravity, orbits perfectly)
- C = Very High (extremely stable)

- They got **compressed** into "Special Case of Relativity"
- At human speeds, they still work perfectly
- But relativity has higher explanatory power

**Phlogiston Theory (1667):**
- E = Moderate (explained combustion somewhat)
- P = Moderate (limited explanatory force)
- C = Moderate (unstable)

When oxygen was discovered:
- Phlogiston had lower explanatory power
- The compression score dropped
- The block didn't compress upward
- **It collapsed completely** and was replaced

**What Triggers a Cascade:**

