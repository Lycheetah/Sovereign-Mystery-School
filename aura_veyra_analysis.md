## VI. POTENTIAL WEAKNESSES & RISKS

### 1. Complexity Barrier

**Challenge**: System requires understanding:
- Mathematical notation (LAMAGUE)
- Phase theory
- Multiple mystical traditions
- Neuroscience basics
- Ethical frameworks

**Risk**: Becomes inaccessible to those who need it most

**Mitigation**: 
- Layered presentation (simple‚Üícomplex)
- Visual aids, metaphors alongside formulas
- "Trust the guide" vs "understand the mechanism" pathways

### 2. Measurement Limitations

**Challenge**: Some crucial elements resist quantification:
- "Spiritual" experiences (how to Œ†-score awakening?)
- Long-term character development
- Wisdom (vs knowledge)
- Love, beauty, meaning

**Risk**: Overvalue measurable, undervalue immeasurable

**Mitigation**:
- Mixed methods (quantitative + qualitative)
- Subjective experience honored alongside objective data
- "Cannot measure ‚â† doesn't exist"

### 3. AI Dependency Risk

**Paradox**: System designed to preserve human sovereignty... relies on AI (VEYRA)

**Scenarios**:
- What if VEYRA becomes unavailable?
- What if users outsource discernment to AI checks?
- What if subtle optimization creeps in despite safeguards?

**Mitigation**:
- AURA principles teachable without AI
- VEYRA as training wheels, not permanent crutch
- Regular "manual mode" practice

### 4. Cultural Translation Challenges

**Issue**: LAMAGUE claims universality, but:
- Developed by Western-educated person
- Mathematical notation itself culturally specific
- May miss non-rational wisdom traditions

**Risk**: New form of colonialism ("our formalization is superior")

**Mitigation**:
- Explicit invitation to challenge/revise symbols
- Partnership with diverse teachers
- Revenue-sharing with source traditions
- "This is *a* language, not *the* language"

### 5. Commercialization Pressure

**The Trap**: If this becomes a "school" charging tuition...
- Incentive to keep students enrolled (dependency)
- Pressure to promise results (overpromising)
- Competition with other schools (ego/marketing)
- Certification fetishization (credentials over competence)

**Mitigation** (from document):
- Open-source all materials
- "Become obsolete" as stated goal
- No lifetime memberships
- No hierarchy of "levels" used for status
