### Practice Components


#### Sitting Meditation
- **Frequency**: 5x per week
- **Duration**: 20 minutes per session
- **Weekly Commitment**: 100 minutes

**Instructions**:
1. Find comfortable seated position
1. Focus on breath at nostrils
1. When mind wanders, gently return to breath
1. No judgment of thoughts or feelings

#### Body Scan
- **Frequency**: 3x per week
- **Duration**: 15 minutes per session
- **Weekly Commitment**: 45 minutes

**Instructions**:
1. Lie down comfortably
1. Systematically scan body from toes to head
1. Notice sensations without changing them
1. Breathe into areas of tension

#### Walking Meditation
- **Frequency**: 2x per week
- **Duration**: 15 minutes per session
- **Weekly Commitment**: 30 minutes

**Instructions**:
1. Walk slowly, mindfully
1. Feel each footstep completely
1. Notice body movement
1. Return attention when it wanders


**Total Program Time Commitment**: 1400 minutes (23.3 hours)
